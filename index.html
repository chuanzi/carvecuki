Carve Cookie Candy Game

A deliciously addictive puzzle game where you slice and carve your way through cookies and candies!

About The Game

Carve Cookie Candy Game is an entertaining puzzle game that challenges your precision and timing. Cut through delicious treats with perfect slices to solve increasingly difficult levels. With colorful graphics and intuitive controls, it's easy to learn but difficult to master!

The game features dozens of unique levels, special power-ups, and various cookie and candy designs that will test your carving skills. Can you become the ultimate cookie carving champion?

How To Play

  • Swipe or drag your cursor to carve through cookies and candies
  • Cut treats into equal parts to pass the level
  • Avoid obstacles and timing challenges in more difficult levels
  • Collect stars and power-ups to boost your score
  • Complete levels to unlock new worlds and challenges
